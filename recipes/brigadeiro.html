Brigadeiro recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 condensed milk box
  • 1 spoon (soup) of margarine without salt
  • 7 chocolate spoons (soup)
  • granulated chocolate

How to make brigadeiro

  1. In a deep pan, add condensed milk, margarine and chocolate powder (or 4 tablespoons of powdered chocolate).
  2. Cook over medium heat and stir until the brigadeiro starts to come away from the pan.
  3. Let it cool and make small balls with your hand by passing the dough into the granulated chocolate.
