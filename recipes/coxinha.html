Coxinha recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of wheat
  • 4 cups of milk
  • 1 chicken broth
  • 1/2 spoon of margarine

How to make brigadeiro

  1. Bring the milk, margarine and broth to a boil.
  2. Let it boil.
  3. Pour the wheat all at once and stir well.
  4. Then remove from the pan and place on the table.
  5. Knead a little and start shaping the drumsticks.
  6. Put it to fry in hot oil, wait for it to brown, remove and serve.
